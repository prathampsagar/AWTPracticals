Menu

  1. Breakfast
    • Maskabun

      All you need is fresh pav or buns, fresh cream and white butter.

    • Milkshake

      • It is usually prepared by milk, ice cream or iced milk, emulsifier and/or stabilizer, and flavorings or sweeteners.


  2. Lunch
    • Chole Bhature

      • It is a combination of chana masala (spicy white chickpeas) and bhatura/puri, a fried bread made from maida.

    • Dal Baati

      • Dal Baati is a popular Rajasthani dish consisting of mainly Uradh Dal (combination of five lentils) and Baati i.e. small wheat bread balls. Baati is dipped in pure ghee and served hot in a traditional earthen pot.


  3. Dinner
    • Jeera Rice

      • Jeera rice is a popular fried rice dish having the flavour of cumin that's easy to make and goes well with any Indian side dish or gravy.

    • Aloo Paratha

      • Aloo paratha are popular Indian flatbreads stuffed with a delicious spiced potato mixture.
